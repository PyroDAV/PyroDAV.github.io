The history of our land is long and truthfully tragic one. You might be familiar with this one. It may appear as a symbol of protest, random combination of 2 colors or even a ultra-nationalistic piece that was forbiden for a more tnan a century. All of that is only partially true in my opinion. For me, "white-red-white" always was and forever will be a sign of freedom, a symbol of purity that is forcefully stained with the blood of the innocent.
