Three most defining traits of the national character

Peacefulness

This is the first feature that is taught to us in childhood - belarusian people are the most peaceful and kind people out there. Sometimes some of us get sceptical if it is true, but any captured footage of recents "actions" shall be the evidence that speaks against any doubts.

Sense of unity

It would be fair to note that this trait is essential to any nation with developed sense of ethnical affiliation, however, I have never seen the kind of unity that Belarusians show these days before. Or maybe I am just biased on that matter)

Friendliness

This kind of goes in one hand with peacefulness, but always gets mentioned on its own, perhaps no less that than the first attribute. If you ever appear on a doorstep of a Belarusian with empty pockets, trust me - you will be treated as if you were a part of a family.
